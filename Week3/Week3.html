19 October 2018

Description of the process:

  1. Conect the Arduino to the computer, in order to program it
  2. Program de Arduino in the computer
  3. Conect the Arduino to a circuit woth LED light with aligator clips to prove it is working.
  4. Place your Attiny in a bread board and then conect it to the Arduino using the cables in the pins
  5. Select the right options for programing the Attiny. Then, you have to look for a tone for your card and paste it in the software to program it in the Attiny.
  6. Design your card usin Adobe Illustrator
  7. Do the sketch of the circuit in the base of the card.
  8. Cover the circuit with Coppertape
  9. Place the LED lights and the speaker. Then solder the conections.
  10. Solder the Speaker, LED lights and battery to the Attiny, in the pins we have already set for each element in the programing process.
  11. Cover the circuit and the conections with tape, in order to make it saver
  12. Make a pocket for the batteries and a botton.
  13. To prepare the visible part of the card, lets cut the elements using Shilouette.
  14. I will make use of the option in which you can print your design in a paper shit using some marks the machine will recognice afterwards.
  15. Make a system that will make the contact with the batteries.
  16. Leave a hole in the speaker so the sound can go out with no difficulties. Then we will cover it with the picture of the radio poping up.
  17. Place all the elements in the card.
  18. Try it out!
